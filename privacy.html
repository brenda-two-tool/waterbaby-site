Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 2026

Thank you for using Water Baby.

Water Baby respects your privacy. This app is designed to work without collecting or transmitting your personal information.

Water Baby is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device.

Water Baby does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Your water intake records, cup size settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device.

Water Baby does not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Water Baby offers an optional one-time in-app purchase through Apple's App Store.

Purchase processing is handled entirely by Apple. Water Baby does not receive your payment information.
